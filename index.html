Solenis Speedometer

This tool will install the Speedometer Firmware on a connected Board to allow Speedmeasurements of RK Instruments Coaters.

Before pressing the "Install"-Button, hold down the "M"-Button on the device and press "R"-Button, than release the "M"-Button.
